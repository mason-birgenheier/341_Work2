Skateboarding: Falling Doesn't Descriminate

When the sun starts to come out, people become more active. Some choose to skateboard for their recreation. Depending on your skill level, you might fall down once or ten times, but until you really get hurt, you won't have a problem. There is an art to falling, but it is inevitable. Just have fun with it!
