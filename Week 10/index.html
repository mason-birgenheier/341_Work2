Responsive Web Design



Responsive Web Design makes websites more user friendly and widely accessible. Making websites easier to use and more visually pleasing on a wider variety of devices could help to increase activity on the site. For example, a potential customer looking at a store's site on their phone or tablet will probably be more likely to make a purchase on the site if it is easy for them to navitage. If a potential customer is having trouble finding an item, logging in, or paying because of the device they are using, they are more likely to go looking on a different company's website. Besides this, a site that is user friendly on any device will feel more professional to customers.
